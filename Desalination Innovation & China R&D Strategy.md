### 2. **Four Core R&D Pillars (Desalination Innovation)**

1. **Digitalization & AI**
    
    - RO performance models already developed.
    - Use of plant data for:
        - Analytics
        - Optimization
        - Predictive operations using AI
    - Goal: improve plant operation, forecasting, and design quality.
2. **Detoxification (Chemical Reduction)**
    
    - Reduce heavy chemical use across desalination processes.
    - Key approaches:
        - CO₂ replacing antiscalants or improving pretreatment coagulation
        - Optimizing CIP to reduce chemical consumption
        - Exploring chemical‑free technologies
    - Aim: lower brine toxicity and environmental impact.
3. **Decarbonization (Energy Reduction)**
    
    - Energy is the largest contributor to water cost.
    - Focus areas:
        - Better pretreatment → cleaner feedwater
        - Next‑gen membranes with lower energy demand
        - AI‑driven operational optimization
    - Objective: reduce total energy consumption and water price.
4. **Emerging Technologies**
    
    - Test startups with **TRL 6–8**.
    - Pilot technologies in **Saudi, UAE**, and potentially China.
    - Goal: validate technologies under real operating conditions.
