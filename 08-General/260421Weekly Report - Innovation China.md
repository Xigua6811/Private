### **1. China Innovation Strategy**

   
- **Hydrogen**: China BD supports the current R&D direction and raised two key needs:
    1. modeling and simulation of **coordinated control for multiple electrolyzers**;
    2. building **in-house hourly simulation tool for capacity sizing** for wind/PV-to-hydrogen projects.
- **Desalination**: R&D demand is strongly focused on **digital O&M such as Robotics to reduce manpower**. Current projects rely on large O&M teams (>200 staff), creating high cost pressure.

### **2. Ongoing R&D Projects**

1. **Drone + EL Inspection**
    
    - Dedicated workshop completed; in-house EL could **reduce costs by ~33%** compared to testing by third-parties.
    - **Next Steps:** Proposal refinement focuses on technical route comparison and quantified savings based on operating PV assets in the Middle East and China.
    
2. **PV Demonstration Plants**
    
    - R&D collaboration intent confirmed with CQC; pilot/demonstration projects planned in Hainan and Xinjiang.
    - **Next steps:** define test collaboration model and sign MOU.
    
3. **Advanced PV Simulation Tools**
    
    - SunSolve identified as key focus, with advantages over PVsyst in bifacial modeling and short-term forecasting. Expected benefits include improved financial accuracy during development and reduced forecasting penalties for operating assets.
    - **Next steps:** Trials with SunSolve and ACWAsim and validation using Ibri II bifacial data are planned.

### **3. Desalination Test Platforms**

- **ISDMU (Tianjin)**:
    - Data & model training – ISDMU can provide local water quality data and some operating data, but data cannot leave the institute, because ISDMU doesn’t own these data and they had NDAs with other parties. 
    - Pilot testing – ISDMU offers access to their 10,000-ton/day pilot facility for pretreatment testing.
    - New research topic – ISDMU is developing fluidized bed technology for mineralization to better handle China’s complex pipe networks and fluctuating water output. The testing platform is ready and in my opinion the result may support our projects in China. 

- **Xinquan Desal Plant**:
    - Limited automation indicating strong potential for digital O&M solutions.
    - Currently it's in TDD process, operating data can be available after Company Acquisition settled.
