


**Weekly Coordination with DRC – Green Hydrogen / Methanol Topics**

### 1. Purpose of the Meeting

- This is a **regular (weekly) coordination call** related to the strategic studies being conducted with the **Development Research Center (DRC)** under China’s State Council.
- The meeting serves as a **channel for BD, R&D, and strategy teams** to:
    - Raise questions, concerns, and research needs related to **hydrogen, methanol, transport, and policy**.
    - Leverage DRC’s expertise, data access, and network to support AquaPower’s China strategy.

### 2. Role and Importance of the DRC

- **DRC is a State Council–affiliated policy think tank**, equivalent in influence to a cabinet-level advisory body.
- It plays a key role in:
    - Supporting formulation of **Five-Year Plans (including the 15th FYP)**.
    - Advising on **energy law, hydrogen policy, desalination policy**, and other national strategies.
- DRC studies are highly influential:
    - Outputs are submitted directly to the **State Council**.
    - In some cases, policy recommendations may reach the Premier level.
- For AquaPower, this cooperation represents:
    - A **high-impact policy influence pathway** in China.
    - A rare and prestigious partnership, typically reserved for large global players.

### 3. Scope of the Joint Studies with DRC

- Two strategic studies are underway:
    1. **Green hydrogen**
    2. **Water desalination**
- Nature of the studies:
    - **Policy-, market-, and industry-oriented**, not pure technical design.
    - Focused on **what China needs in terms of policies, incentives, and market structures**, benchmarked against international practices.
- Objective:
    - Enable long-term growth of hydrogen and desalination markets in China.
    - Create a favorable policy environment for future business development.

    - Support **ad-hoc questions** that arise from day-to-day business needs.
- Example cited:
